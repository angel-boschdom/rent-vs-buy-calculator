Optimal Time to Buy a House

You are renting now, and you have some savings. Would you be better off buying a house in the long term, or keep renting instead? How long should you wait before buying the house? This calculator helps you answer this question.

How to Use This Calculator

Fill the form below, and click "Plot Results". The graph shows your long-term net worth vs how many years you wait before buying the house.

Pay attention to the maximum net worth in the graph. If the maximum net worth is at the end of the time horizon, this means that you are better off renting the entire period and not buying a house at all.

If the maximum net worth is at the beginning of the time horizon, this means you are better off buying the house as soon as possible.

If the maximum net worth is somewhere in between, that is the optimal time to wait before buying the house.
